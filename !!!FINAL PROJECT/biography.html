All About Me

My name is Connor, and I am a 22-year-old transfer student currently studying for my BA in Music Performance with a Minor in Web Development. I originally went to Michigan Tech back in the Fall of 2020, but that didn't end very well...

I play the Bass Trombone in various Ensembles on campus, including the Jazz Band and Wind Symphony. I've been playing the Trombone since 5th grade and picked up the Bass Trombone back in my sophomore year of high school when I joined Jazz Band for the first time.

I began learning how to program back in Middle School when my parents purchased me a subscription to Youth Digital where I took a course on how to make my own Minecraft mod. After that, my interest primarily turned to that of making a bot for the platform Discord, which you can find more information about here.

I'm a big gamer, and I really enjoy esports. My game of choice is Overwatch, so when arriving to both MTU and Point I inquired about the esports programs each had. While I was placed on a sort of temporary position with MTU, I have played for both UWSP's Purple and Gold Overwatch teams. In fact, this semester I was able to broadcast our Purple team winning their nationals match!

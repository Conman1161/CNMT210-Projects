Yup, it's gamer time.

I am a very competitive person when it comes to Overwatch. I have been playing the game since approximately 2018, and I've been big into the competitive mode since. To date, I have 1,096 hours in the competitive game mode alone. You can see my rank history from Overwatch 2 on the right. Unfortunately, while the data from Overwatch 1 is still used to analyze statistics, the competitive history has been cleared for any season prior to Overwatch 2.

In my Senior year of High School, I formed the Shawano High School Esports program. At the time, we were only able to play Rocket League and League of Legends due to hardware limitations and rules from the Wisconsin High School Esports Association (WIHSEA) station all players had to play from school. While we didn't place well that year, and with COVID happening the following semester, that chapter of my career closed almost as quick as it began.

After COVID happened, school came to a halt and I was unable to compete until I arrived at Michigan Tech. While I didn't make the cut originally in Fall, I was brought in during the Spring semester to play Main Tank. We only had one major tournament, the Michigan Bandwidth Bowl, but we ended with a strong 2nd place, which I'm very proud of considering it was an off-role and I had teammates who were much better than me at the time.

Once the nightmare of COVID began to roll away, I transferred to UWSP and immediately took an interest in growing the Esports program on campus. In my first semester at point, I became Vice-President of our campus' Esports program. During that initial year, I helped build a foundation for the program to grow. The next fall, Tristan (the then-President) had to take a leave and I was promoted to President and had a lot of things to manage. After a very long semester, I relieved myself from that position and took a less-intense role of Production Manager. My current role has me streaming most, but preferably all, of our weekly matches for anyone to view. These streams happen at twitch.tv/uwsp_esports, so if you're interested in watching feel free to stop by. We've got big plans to remodel the page for Fall!

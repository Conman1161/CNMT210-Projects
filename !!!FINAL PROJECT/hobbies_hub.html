Here are some of my hobbies!

I have several hobbies, including Yu-Gi-Oh!, Overwatch, and Music. Yu-Gi-Oh! (YGO) is a 1v1 duel game where you create a deck with trading cards you'd acquired to reduce your opponent's life points to 0.

Overwatch is a 5v5 team-based First-Person Shooter where you main goal is to capture an objective and progress it to 100% completion.

I love music. I have been playing the Trombone since I was in 5th grade and it has helped me through tough times. I'm also a fan of electronic music and artists such as Madeon and Porter Robinson.

If I had tot rank my hobbies by how much I enjoy them, this would be the order:

  1. Music
  2. Overwatch
  3. Yu-Gi-Oh!

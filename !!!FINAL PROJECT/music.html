Music, music, music!

My first main exposure to performing music was all the way back in 5th grade, where I began playing the Tenor Trombone. Some of my earliest memories from band were in the RCI Band Room, including my teacher praising me from performing and practicing in front of the class and asking me how much I practice (I didn't really practice...)

I continued playing trombone to this day, but was introduced to playing the Bass Trombone during my Sophomore year in high school. After only playing for a few months, I feel in love with the range that the Bass Trombone offered compared to that of the tenor.

In the past few years, I've also gained an interest in electronic music, especially music by both Madeon and Porter Robinson. Both musicians have awards credited to them for their work, and have released albums to critical acclaim on several occasions. If you're looking for suggestions, I would recommend Madeon's Good Faith Album along with Porter Robinson's newest single, KNOCK YOURSELF OUT XD, from his upcoming album (listen below!)

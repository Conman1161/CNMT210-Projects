Grrrreetings!

I am a big fan of the game Overwatch, a first-person shooter with two teams of 5 with the primary objective to capture objectives. The game, made by Blizzard Entertainment, originally released in May of 2016 with two teams of 6, but re-released at Overwatch 2 in October of 2022 with many large changes, along with reducing the team size to 5.

The Overwatch League, previously Blizzard's premier Overwatch Esports tournament, ran between December 2017 and October 2023. While not the first esports league for Overwatch, it served as the primary league for any player trying to make it in the Overwatch scene. A set number of organizations were allowed to own teams, based and named after various locations (Atlanta Reign, Chengdu Hunters, New York Excelsior, etc.). The final victor of the Overwatch League (Season 6) were the Florida Mayhem, winning $1M in prizing.

Currently, the Premier Overwatch Esports Circuit is run by FACEIT in the Overwatch Champions Series (OWCS). UWSP Takes place in NECC'S tournament, which is separate from OWCS. This past semester, we had two teams: Purple and Gold. I played on our Gold team, which made it to 3rd place in Playoffs. Our Purple team made it all the way to Finals in Nationals and won it 4-2! You can watch the recording of that Grand Finals match on our YouTube channel.

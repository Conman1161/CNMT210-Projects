It's Time to Duel!

Yu-Gi-Oh! (YGO) is a Trading Card Game where players collect cards in order to build a deck of 40-60 cards. Players face off in duels, where the primary goal is to reduce your opponent's Life Points from 8000 to 0. Different decks have different play styles, or ways their cards interact with each other, and have drastically different ways to beat your opponent.

The archetype (a group of cards build around a similar theme) I play are called Libromancers, with the main theme being summoning book characters from their books in order to fight evil. While the deck isn't good per se, I enjoy playing them and enjoy the time in the community.

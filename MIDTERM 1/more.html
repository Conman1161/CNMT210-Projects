Terry's Inspiration

Terrence's overall design is based off of the "Varied Bunting", a bird native to Arizona, New Mexico, and Texas, along with Mexico.

The colors are certainly exotic, which make for a very interesting design when drawing. I enjoy Terry because his design is very unique, and I enjoy my friends' characters.

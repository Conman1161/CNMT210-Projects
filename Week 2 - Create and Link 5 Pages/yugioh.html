Set the scales in Yu-Gi-Oh!


Yu-Gi-Oh! is a Trading Card Game where two players compete to reduce the other's life points from 8000 to 0. Cards come pre-packaged in packs of 9 and it is up to chance which cards you open. You are able to use any of the over 10,000 cards that have been created in you deck, unless it is part of a special Forbidden and Limited list. Gamers get together every weekend to compete against each other, with the winners getting various prizes. It's a lot of fun, get your game on!

Mannadium Prime-Heart has a special defensive ability that stops your opponent from targeting it with card effects. No monsters facing Mannadium Prime-Heart will be safe due to its multiple energy blades! Find this powerful card in Cyberstorm Access, releasing on May 5th!

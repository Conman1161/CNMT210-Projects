The History of Valentines day

February 14 is called Valentine’s Day because the Roman Catholic Church honors two saints named Valentine on that day. Both Saint Valentines were ancient Romans of the ad 200s who died for their Christian faith. Groups of young men and women once celebrated Valentine’s Day by picking up valentines from a box.
